Usage Rights

Free Usage

The following users can use pyHaasAPI for free:

  • Individual Traders: Personal trading, educational purposes, non-commercial research

  • Research Institutions: Academic research, university projects, non-profit organizations

  • Open Source Projects: Projects that are open source and non-commercial

Commercial Licensing

Commercial licensing is required for:

  • Hedge Funds: Any hedge fund or investment fund using the library

  • Financial Institutions: Banks, brokerages, and other financial services companies

  • Commercial Trading Firms: Companies that trade for profit or provide trading services

  • White Label Solutions: Reselling or rebranding the library as part of a commercial product

We offer flexible partnership arrangements including:

  • Custom integrations and features

  • Priority support and maintenance

  • Advanced analytics and reporting

  • Training and consultation services

  • White label licensing

Contact Information

For licensing inquiries or commercial partnerships:

When using pyHaasAPI, please include appropriate attribution:
